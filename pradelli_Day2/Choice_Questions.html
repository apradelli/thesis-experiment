At the end of the experiment, you will be matched randomly with another player to determine your payoff in the second day. Therefore, your final payoff will also depend on whether the other player plays Task 3 or Task 4, but not on whether she succeeds in the task. The payoff structure is:

  • If you and your partner play Task 4, you will gain the payoff of Task 4 plus a bonus of 40 ECU.
  • If you play Task 4 and your partner plays Task 3, you will gain the payoff of Task 4 minus a malus of 40 ECU, while she will gain the payoff of Task 3 plus a bonus of 100.
  • If you play Task 3 and your partner plays Task 4, you will gain the payoff of Task 3 plus a bonus of 100 ECU, while she will gain the payoff of Task 4 minus a malus of 40.
  • If both of you play Task 3, you will gain the payoff of Task 3 without any bonus or malus.
